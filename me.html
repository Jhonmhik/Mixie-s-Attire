ABOUT

Welcome to Mixie's Jersey, your one-stop shop for top-quality athletic jerseys for badminton, basketball, and volleyball! We are passionate about sports and understand the importance of looking and feeling your best on the court.

Our Story

Mixie's Jersey was founded by jhon Mhiko Merina, a lifelong athlete who recognized the need for stylish and functional jerseys at an affordable price. Tired of limited options and overpriced gear, jhon Mhiko Merina set out to create a brand that catered to all levels of players, from enthusiastic beginners to seasoned veterans.
